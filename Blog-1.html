What makes a space unique?



SÈL | Project King St| Living room

Hello, wonderful readers! If you've landed here, you're likely either a curious client wondering about the frequent use of the word "unique" in designers' presentations, or you're a fellow designer like me who has wondered what exactly makes a space unique. How can we inject that special touch into our designs? Let's delve into this topic together and uncover the secrets of "Unique."

In this exploration, we'll unravel what it takes to turn a space into something truly exceptional, enchanting, and yes, truly one-of-a-kind. Plus, I'll be sharing some valuable tips on how you can infuse that magical quality into your own space. So, let's roll up our sleeves and discover some fantastic insights!


Express yourself!

It's all about YOUR space – create an environment that truly resonates with you. But now you might be wondering, how exactly? Well, here's the scoop: let's talk about those essential elements. Whether it's a standout furniture item or a collection of decorations, think about what speaks to you. It could be a pop of your favorite color, a gallery wall proudly featuring your treasured Taylor Swift albums (yes you can!), or perhaps your green thumb's love for plants. Seriously, it can be anything that mirrors your personality and style.

Credits | Pinterest

Using that space creatively!

Here's the crux of the matter! How are you making the most of your space? Consider that corner you've been neglecting, especially if your room setup is a bit different. Rather than crowding it with furniture, opt for a versatile piece that serves multiple purposes, giving you more room to breathe. And if you're blessed with high ceilings, don't let that space go to waste—utilize the vertical dimension to your advantage.

Play with materials!


Credits | Pinterest

Let's dive in with a burst of creativity! Mix and match various textures, patterns, and colors, along with some plush materials, to craft a vibe that's truly your own. Get adventurous by giving that wall a fresh coat of paint, introducing captivating patterns, or even considering trendy wallpapers that'll take your space up a notch. Experimenting with different textures and layering materials not only adds dimension but also amps up the charm. Take that classic Ikea cabinet, for instance – paint it a bold blue, switch up the frame's color, and jazz up the handles. The possibilities are abundant, and here's the cherry on top – it won't break the bank!

Lit up those lights!

In the world of design, lighting is a game-changer, and it's more than just ambiance – it's a daily life enhancer. As a designer, I consistently stress the importance of not overlooking the lighting aspect. It's just as crucial as any other design element. Small changes, like a charming lampshade, can make a big impact. Don't underestimate the power of swapping out an outdated pot light for an elegant chandelier. And let's not forget about the light temperature. It's a crucial factor. For instance, cool or daylight tones are perfect for tasks demanding high attention, while warm white sets a cozy, relaxing vibe. Neutral light steps in where visibility is a priority without being overly warm or cool. And here's a gem – LED lights. They're not just energy-efficient and eco-friendly, but they're also customizable. You can adjust their temperature according to your needs, adding that extra touch of personalization.

Let’s bring down the Curtains!

Yes! When it comes to the final flourish, curtains are the game-changer. Believe me, they have the power to completely transform a room's appearance. If you've got the luxury of high ceilings, don't let that space go to waste – embrace it! Opt for those elegant, floor-length curtains. They not only infuse character but also bring a touch of drama and that much-needed uniqueness. Another trick up our sleeve? Layering. Mix it up by combining sheer and heavy drapes for a captivating, multidimensional effect. Your windows are ready to steal the spotlight!
