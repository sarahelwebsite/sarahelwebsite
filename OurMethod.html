P H A S E 2 : T H E C O N S U L T A T I O N
Do you want to know us? We want to know about you too! (Excited) This is the phase where the designer would meet the client on their site where we would discuss everything in depth to know about you and your style, understand each room and go around with the client's, measurements of the space, and ask questions in depth like how do they want to feel when they enter into the space? What's their vision for the next 5-10 years with their home? do they want to prioritize their home in stages? and many more, also start building trust and friendship to best work collaboratively with each other and have fun creating a beautiful space.
"FOR A SEAMLESS AND FUN PROCESS WE HAVE PHASES FOR EVERY PROJECT"

P H A S E 3 : THEDESIGN PHASE
This next phase is where the designer will be prepping up with the whole design package which includes floor plans, elevations, millwork drawings, mood boards, basic 3d renders (if any), setting up a material library for each space for the client to touch and feel it, and a budget review. Once this is set up, this all will be presented to the client.

P H A S E 4: PROJECT MANAGEMENT
To have your dream space ready with the quality of work in detail for small or large projects is a lot of work and we value your time. We handle everything from ordering items, taking care of the deliveries, problem- solving at the site, and coordinating with the trades, installs and assemblies.

P H A S E 5 : T H E F I N A L R E V E A L
And yes FINALLY! THE EXECUTION. The most awaited stage for the clients to now see their dream space. The whole project is then executed with the help of our vendors who are involved in turning the client's vision into a beautiful reality.
